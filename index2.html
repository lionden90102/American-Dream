Carlos Santana – Summary

Overview

* Full Name: Carlos Humberto Santana Barragán

* Born: July 20, 1947, in Autlán de Navarro, Jalisco, Mexico

* Occupation: American guitarist; founder of the band Santana

Notable Achievements:

* Blended rock, blues, and Latin American jazz

* Ranked #11 greatest guitarist (2023) by Rolling Stone

* 10 Grammy Awards and 3 Latin Grammy Awards

* Inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame (1998)
