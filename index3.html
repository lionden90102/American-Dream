Early Life

* Learned violin at 5, guitar at 8, taught by his mariachi father.

* Moved from Autlán → Tijuana → San Francisco.

* Early exposure to rock and blues via local band The Tj’s and mentor Javier Bátiz.

* Influenced by artists like B.B. King, T-Bone Walker, Muddy Waters, and James Brown.

* Strongly inspired by Gábor Szabó, Hendrix, Bloomfield, Peter Green, and Miles Davis.

* Left Mission High School (1965); declined college offers to pursue music.
